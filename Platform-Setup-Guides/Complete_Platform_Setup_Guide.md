## Part 2: Platforms You Must Set Up Yourself (DIY)

For these platforms, no specialized setup services exist. The registration processes are straightforward, and you are expected to create your own profile. 

**Your Strategy:** Use the professionally written content from your Upwork and LinkedIn profiles (created by the experts) as a template for these platforms.

### Freelance & Job Platforms

*   **Braintrust:** 
    *   **How to Set Up:** Follow the official [21 Profile Tips From A Braintrust Hiring Manager](https://www.usebraintrust.com/blog/21-profile-tips). The guide is comprehensive and provides everything you need to create a winning profile.
*   **Toptal:**
    *   **How to Set Up:** You must pass their rigorous 5-step screening process on your own. No contractor can help you with this. Focus on your technical skills and communication.
*   **FlexJobs & HigherEdJobs:**
    *   **How to Set Up:** These are traditional job boards. You don't create a detailed profile; you apply to jobs directly with your resume. Use the enhanced resume we created for you.

### Tutoring & Mentoring Platforms

*   **Wyzant, MentorCruise, Chegg:**
    *   **How to Set Up:** All three have simple, self-service registration processes. You will need to fill out your subject expertise, write a bio (use your LinkedIn summary as a base), and set your hourly rate.

### Course Creation Platforms (Alternatives to Teachable)

*   **Udemy & Coursera:**
    *   **Udemy:** Udemy is a DIY platform. They provide a free [Official Udemy Course on How to Create a Course](https://www.udemy.com/course/official-udemy-create-course/).
    *   **Coursera:** You cannot create courses as an individual. Coursera only partners with universities and large organizations.

### Virtual Assistant Platforms

*   **BELAY:**
    *   **How to Set Up:** BELAY is not a freelance marketplace; it's a company that hires virtual assistants. You must go through their internal, multi-step hiring process, which includes tests and interviews. No external service can help with this.
