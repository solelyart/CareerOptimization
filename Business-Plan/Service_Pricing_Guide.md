# Momentum Ops Consulting: Service Pricing & ROI Guide

## How to Price Your Services and Justify Value to Clients

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Your pricing should reflect three key factors:

1.  **Your Expertise:** 5+ years of experience with proven results (35% adoption increases, 40% efficiency gains)
2.  **Market Rates:** Salesforce consultants command $100-200+/hour
3.  **Client ROI:** Your services should deliver 5-10x return on investment

**Golden Rule:** Price based on value delivered, not hours worked.

## Pricing Negotiation Framework

### When a Client Says "That's Too Expensive"

**Response Strategy:**
1.  **Acknowledge:** "I understand budget is a concern."
2.  **Reframe:** "Let's look at this as an investment, not an expense."
3.  **Quantify ROI:** "If we can save your team 15 hours/week, that's $39,000/year in productivity gains."
4.  **Offer Alternatives:** "We could start with a Health Check to identify the highest-impact areas."

### Payment Terms Options

**Standard:**
- 50% upfront, 50% on completion

**For Larger Projects:**
- 33% upfront, 33% at midpoint, 34% on completion

**For Retainers:**
- Monthly billing on the 1st of each month
- 30-day cancellation notice required

## When to Raise Your Rates

**Indicators It's Time:**
- You're booked 4+ weeks in advance
- You're turning down projects due to capacity
- You have 3+ strong testimonials
- You've completed 10+ successful projects

**How Much to Raise:**
- Year 1 → Year 2: 10-15% increase
- Year 2 → Year 3: 15-20% increase
- Existing retainer clients: Grandfather for 6-12 months, then increase

Based on similar projects, clients typically see [specific ROI metric] within [timeframe]. This represents a [X]x return on your investment. Does this align with your budget and expected outcomes?"
