# 10-Hour VA Trial: Time Allocation and Workflow (Updated)

**Objective:** To strategically utilize a 10-hour trial period with a virtual assistant (VA) from Time Etc. to complete the highest priority platform profile setups.

**Priority Tiers (Updated):**

*   **CRITICAL (1):** Braintrust
*   **HIGH (0):** 
*   **MEDIUM (7):** Toptal, Indeed, FlexJobs, Wyzant, MentorCruise, Udemy, Teachable
*   **LOW (6):** LinkedIn Services Marketplace, HigherEdJobs, Chegg, Coursera, BELAY, UMGC

**Workflow (Updated):**

1.  **Hour 0-1: Onboarding and Initial Review (1 hour)**
    *   VA reviews the full contents of the VA Trial Folder.
    *   VA reviews the Time Allocation Guide and the instructions for the first two platforms (Braintrust and Toptal).
    *   VA asks any clarifying questions before beginning.

2.  **Hour 1-3.5: Braintrust Profile (2.5 hours)**
    *   VA creates and optimizes the Braintrust profile.

3.  **Hour 3.5-5: Toptal Application (1.5 hours)**
    *   VA completes the Toptal application.

4.  **Hour 5-6: Indeed Profile (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates and optimizes the Indeed profile.

5.  **Hour 6-6.5: FlexJobs Profile (0.5 hours)**
    *   VA creates the FlexJobs profile.

6.  **Hour 6.5-7.5: Wyzant Profile (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates the Wyzant tutor profile.

7.  **Hour 7.5-8.5: MentorCruise Profile (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates the MentorCruise mentor profile.

8.  **Hour 8.5-9: Udemy Instructor Account (0.5 hours)**
    *   VA creates the Udemy instructor account and course shell.

9.  **Hour 9-10: Teachable School and Course Shell (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates the Teachable school and course shell.

10. **Final Review and Deliverables (within the 10 hours)**
    *   VA completes the Master Credentials Spreadsheet for the completed platforms.
    *   VA completes the Profile Links Report for the completed platforms.
    *   VA provides a brief summary of the work completed.
