# 10-Hour VA Trial: Time Allocation and Workflow

**Objective:** To strategically utilize a 10-hour trial period with a virtual assistant (VA) from Time Etc. to complete the highest priority platform profile setups.

**Priority Tiers:**

*   **CRITICAL (1):** LinkedIn Services Marketplace
*   **HIGH (1):** Braintrust
*   **MEDIUM (7):** Toptal, Indeed, FlexJobs, Wyzant, MentorCruise, Udemy, Teachable
*   **LOW (5):** HigherEdJobs, Chegg, Coursera, BELAY, UMGC

**Time Allocation (10 Hours):**

| Priority | Platform | Estimated Time (Hours) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| CRITICAL | LinkedIn Services Marketplace | 2.5 |
| HIGH | Braintrust | 2.0 |
| MEDIUM | Toptal | 1.5 |
| MEDIUM | Indeed | 1.0 |
| MEDIUM | FlexJobs | 0.5 |
| MEDIUM | Wyzant | 1.0 |
| MEDIUM | MentorCruise | 1.0 |
| MEDIUM | Udemy | 0.5 |
| **Total** | | **10.0** |

**Workflow:**

1.  **Hour 0-1: Onboarding and Initial Review (1 hour)**
    *   VA reviews the full contents of the VA Trial Folder.
    *   VA reviews the Time Allocation Guide and the instructions for the first two platforms (LinkedIn and Braintrust).
    *   VA asks any clarifying questions before beginning.

2.  **Hour 1-3.5: LinkedIn Profile and Services Marketplace (2.5 hours)**
    *   VA completes the main LinkedIn profile optimization.
    *   VA creates the LinkedIn Services Marketplace page.

3.  **Hour 3.5-5.5: Braintrust Profile (2 hours)**
    *   VA creates and optimizes the Braintrust profile.

4.  **Hour 5.5-7: Toptal Application (1.5 hours)**
    *   VA completes the Toptal application.

5.  **Hour 7-8: Indeed Profile (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates and optimizes the Indeed profile.

6.  **Hour 8-8.5: FlexJobs Profile (0.5 hours)**
    *   VA creates the FlexJobs profile.

7.  **Hour 8.5-9.5: Wyzant Profile (1 hour)**
    *   VA creates the Wyzant tutor profile.

8.  **Hour 9.5-10: Final Review and Deliverables (0.5 hours)**
    *   VA completes the Master Credentials Spreadsheet for the completed platforms.
    *   VA completes the Profile Links Report for the completed platforms.
    *   VA provides a brief summary of the work completed.
