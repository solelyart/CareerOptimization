# Wyzant Profile Setup (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Objective:** Create a warm and approachable tutor profile on Wyzant to attract students.

**Required Elements:**

*   **Tutor Profile:** Complete all sections of the tutor profile.
*   **Professional Bio:** Write a 300-500 word bio that is warm and approachable.
*   **Subjects:** List 3-5 subjects with detailed descriptions:
    *   Business Systems Analysis
    *   Salesforce Administration
    *   Agile/Scrum
    *   SQL/Databases
    *   Career Coaching for Tech
*   **Hourly Rate:** Set the hourly rate to $60-100/hour, or as directed by the client.
*   **Availability Calendar:** Configure the availability calendar.
*   **Education:** Complete the education section.
*   **Background Check:** Initiate the background check if required by the platform.

**Tone:** Approachable, helpful, and student-focused.

**Success Criteria:**

*   Profile is live.
*   Calendar is open for bookings.
