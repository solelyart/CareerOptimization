# Chegg Tutor Application (Priority: LOW)

**Objective:** Submit a complete and accurate tutor application to Chegg.

**Required Elements:**

*   **Tutor Application:** Complete and submit the tutor application.
*   **Subject Expertise:** Select all relevant subject expertise areas.
*   **Academic Credentials:** Upload all academic credentials.
*   **ID Verification:** Complete the ID verification process.
*   **Profile Photo:** Upload a professional profile photo.

**Important Note:**

Chegg has an internal approval process that can take 1-2 weeks. Your role is to complete the application accurately. The approval outcome is outside of your control.

**Success Criteria:**

*   Application is submitted and awaiting Chegg approval.
