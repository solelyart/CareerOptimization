# UMGC Adjunct Faculty Application (Priority: LOW)

**Objective:** Submit a complete and professional application for an adjunct faculty position at the University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC).

**Required Elements:**

*   **Adjunct Faculty Application:** Complete the application.
*   **CV Upload:** Upload the resume in an academic CV format.
*   **Cover Letter:** Express interest in teaching Information Systems courses.
*   **Teaching Philosophy Statement:** Write a 200-300 word teaching philosophy statement.
*   **Transcripts:** Upload transcripts (the client will provide these).
*   **References:** Provide contact information for references (the client will provide this).
*   **Desired Courses/Subjects:** Indicate desired courses/subjects:
    *   Business Systems Analysis
    *   Information Systems Management
    *   Agile Project Management
    *   Database Management

**Success Criteria:**

*   Application is complete and submitted.
