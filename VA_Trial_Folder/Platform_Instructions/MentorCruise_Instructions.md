# MentorCruise Mentor Application (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Objective:** Create a supportive and guiding mentor profile on MentorCruise to attract mentees.

**Required Elements:**

*   **Mentor Application:** Complete the mentor application.
*   **Mentor Bio:** Write a 400-600 word bio focused on mentorship.
*   **Areas of Expertise:** Clearly define all areas of expertise.
*   **Mentorship Offerings:** Describe mentorship offerings and packages.
*   **Pricing:** Set pricing at $80-120/hour or as monthly packages.
*   **Availability:** Configure your availability.

**Tone:** Mentor-mentee relationship, supportive, and guiding.

**Success Criteria:**

*   Profile is live and accepting mentee applications.
