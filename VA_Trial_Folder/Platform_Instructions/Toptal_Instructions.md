# Toptal Application (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Objective:** Submit a highly polished and strategic application to Toptal to maximize the chances of passing their initial screening.

**Required Elements:**

*   **Application Form:** Complete all fields of the application form.
*   **Professional Overview:** Write a 500+ word professional overview that is highly polished and strategic.
*   **Technical Skills:** Accurately list all relevant technical skills.
*   **Portfolio:** Select and showcase the top 3-5 strongest projects.
*   **Application Questions:** Answer all application questions thoroughly and thoughtfully.

**Important Note:**

Toptal has a rigorous 5-step screening process. Your role is to optimize the application to the highest possible standard. The client understands that the screening outcome is outside of your control, and there is no penalty if the client does not pass Toptal's internal assessments.

**Success Criteria:**

*   Application is submitted with the highest quality content.
