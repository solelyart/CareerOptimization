### 3. Certification Timing (Controversial Insight!)

**Start earning BEFORE certifying:**

- Salesforce Admin cert costs $240 + 60 hours study
- That's 60 hours you could spend earning $80/hr = $4,800
- Cert might increase rate by $10-20/hr
- Payback period: 12-24 billable hours

**Better approach:**
- Month 1-2: Focus 100% on landing first clients (no studying)
- Month 3-4: Once earning $2,000+/month, THEN study
- Month 5-6: Take exam after income buffer established

**Exception:** If you're not getting responses from clients after 50 proposals, then certifications become higher priority.
