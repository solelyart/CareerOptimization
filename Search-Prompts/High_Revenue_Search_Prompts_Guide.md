# The Ultimate Guide to High-Revenue Search Prompts

This guide provides a comprehensive playbook of the most effective search prompts and targeting formulas to find high-value clients for your Salesforce consultancy. These prompts are designed for platforms like Apollo.io and LinkedIn Sales Navigator.

---

## The Core Principle: The Prospecting Formula

Successful prospecting isn't about a single search; it's about combining multiple data points to create a highly specific, high-intent audience. The core formula is:

**[Company Fit] + [Decision-Maker Persona] + [Buying Signal] = High-Value Prospect**

- **Company Fit:** Does the company match your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)? (Industry, Size, Revenue, Technology)
- **Decision-Maker Persona:** Is this the right person to talk to? (Job Title, Seniority)
- **Buying Signal:** Why should they buy *now*? (Hiring, Funding, Growth, Bad Reviews)

## Tier 1: The 5 Most Profitable Search Prompts

These are your bread-and-butter searches. They are designed to find companies that have a clear and immediate need for Salesforce optimization.

### 1. The "Scaling Pain" Prompt

**Goal:** Find fast-growing companies whose systems are likely breaking.

**Formula:** `(Company Headcount Growth > 20%) + (Industry = SaaS OR B2B Services) + (Employees = 50-500) + (Job Title = VP of Sales OR VP of Sales Operations)`

| Platform | Filters to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Apollo.io** | **Company:** Headcount Growth (20-50%), Industry (Computer Software, IT Services), # of Employees (51-200, 201-500) <br> **People:** Job Titles (VP of Sales, VP of Sales Operations, Director of Sales Operations) |
| **LinkedIn** | **Company:** Company Headcount Growth (20%+), Industry (Software Development, IT Services), Company Size (51-200, 201-500) <br> **Lead:** Title (VP of Sales, VP of Sales Operations, Director of Sales Operations) |

**Why It Works:** Rapid growth is the #1 reason companies need to optimize Salesforce. Their processes are breaking, their data is a mess, and they are losing deals due to inefficiency.

### 2. The "New Hire, New Budget" Prompt

**Goal:** Find new executives who want to make an impact (and have a budget to do so).

**Formula:** `(Job Title = VP of Sales OR CRO) + (Time in Current Role < 6 months) + (Company uses Salesforce) + (Employees = 50-500)`

| Platform | Filters to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Apollo.io** | **People:** Job Titles (VP of Sales, Chief Revenue Officer), Time in Current Role (0-3 months, 3-6 months) <br> **Company:** Technologies (Salesforce) |
| **LinkedIn** | **Lead:** Title (VP of Sales, Chief Revenue Officer), People with new jobs in the past 90 days <br> **Account:** Use Salesforce (via tech stack data) |

**Why It Works:** New leaders are hired to make changes. They have a mandate to improve performance and a budget to hire consultants who can help them get quick wins.

### 3. The "We're Hiring for Your Job" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies that are trying to solve their Salesforce problems with a full-time hire, but could be convinced to use a consultant instead.

**Formula:** `(Active Job Posting Title = Salesforce Administrator OR Salesforce Business Analyst) + (Industry = SaaS) + (Employees = 50-250)`

| Platform | Filters to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Apollo.io** | **Company:** Job Postings (Title: Salesforce Administrator, Salesforce Business Analyst), Industry (Computer Software) |
| **LinkedIn** | **Company:** Actively hiring for "Salesforce Administrator" or "Salesforce Business Analyst" |

**Why It Works:** A job posting is a public declaration of pain. You can approach them with a compelling offer: "Get the results you want in 30 days for a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire, without the recruiting hassle."

### 4. The "Recent Funding" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies with fresh cash to invest in growth and operations.

**Formula:** `(Funding Date in last 6 months) + (Funding Amount > $5M) + (Industry = SaaS) + (Job Title = COO OR VP of Sales)`

| Platform | Filters to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Apollo.io** | **Company:** Funding (Last 6 months, Series A, B, or C) <br> **People:** Job Titles (Chief Operating Officer, VP of Sales) |
| **LinkedIn** | **Company:** Recent funding events (via news alerts) |

**Why It Works:** Companies that just raised a round are under immense pressure to scale. They have the budget and the mandate to invest in systems that will drive growth.

### 5. The "Bad Tech Stack" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies using a competitor's CRM who might be unhappy, or companies using complementary tech that indicates a need for integration.

**Formula:** `(Technologies = HubSpot CRM OR Zoho CRM) AND (Technologies != Salesforce) + (Industry = B2B Services) + (Job Title = VP of Sales)`

| Platform | Filters to Use |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Apollo.io** | **Company:** Technologies (Include: HubSpot, Zoho CRM; Exclude: Salesforce) <br> **People:** Job Titles (VP of Sales) |
| **LinkedIn** | **Company:** Technologies (via company page or employee profiles) |

**Why It Works:** This allows you to run a competitive displacement campaign, highlighting the limitations of their current CRM and the benefits of migrating to Salesforce.

## Tier 2: Advanced & Niche Search Prompts

Use these prompts to uncover hidden opportunities and target specific pain points.

### 6. The "Low User Adoption" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies where Salesforce is likely being underutilized.

**Formula:** `(Industry = Manufacturing OR Logistics) + (Employees = 200-1000) + (Salesforce Users < 10% of Employees) + (Job Title = CIO OR Director of IT)`

**Why It Works:** In non-tech industries, Salesforce adoption is often low. This prompt identifies companies that have invested in the platform but are not getting the ROI, making them prime candidates for an optimization project.

### 7. The "RevOps Transition" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies that are building a Revenue Operations (RevOps) function and need to align their systems.

**Formula:** `(Job Title = Director of Revenue Operations OR VP of RevOps) + (Time in Current Role < 1 year) + (Industry = SaaS)`

**Why It Works:** The rise of RevOps is a huge opportunity. These leaders are tasked with unifying Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success data, which almost always requires a significant Salesforce project.

### 8. The "M&A Integration" Prompt

**Goal:** Find companies that have recently been acquired or have acquired another company, creating a need to merge Salesforce instances.

**Formula:** `(News = Merger OR Acquisition in last 6 months) + (Job Title = CIO OR Director of Business Systems)`

**Why It Works:** Merging two Salesforce orgs is a complex and high-value project that companies almost always outsource to expert consultants.

## How to Use These Prompts: A 30-Day Action Plan

### Week 1: Build Your Core Lists

1.  **Sign up for Apollo.io's free plan.**
2.  **Build and save searches** for the top 3 most profitable prompts: "Scaling Pain," "New Hire, New Budget," and "We're Hiring for Your Job."
3.  **Create a list** for each search.

### Week 2: Launch Your First Campaign

1.  **Select your first list** (start with "Scaling Pain").
2.  **Find verified emails** for the first 50 prospects.
3.  **Write a personalized email sequence** that speaks directly to their pain point (e.g., "Saw you're growing at 30% YoY - congrats! Usually, this is when Salesforce starts to feel the strain...").
4.  **Launch the sequence** using Apollo's built-in tools.

### Week 3: Analyze and Iterate

1.  **Track your open rates, reply rates, and positive reply rates.**
2.  **Analyze the responses.** What objections are you getting? What resonates?
3.  **Refine your email copy** based on the feedback.
4.  **Launch a campaign** for your second list ("New Hire, New Budget").

### Week 4: Scale and Expand

1.  **Double down on the winning prompts.** If one list is performing significantly better than others, focus your efforts there.
2.  **Upgrade to a paid Apollo plan** to increase your sending volume.
3.  **Start experimenting with the Tier 2 prompts** to uncover new niches.

By systematically working through these high-revenue search prompts, you will build a predictable and scalable pipeline of high-value clients for your Salesforce consultancy.
