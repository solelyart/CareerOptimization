# Interview Questions for Platform Setup Contractors

**Purpose:** Use this comprehensive question bank to thoroughly vet contractors before hiring them for your 15-platform profile setup project.

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Pre-Screen:** Use Section 1 questions in your initial message
2. **Interview:** If they pass pre-screen, schedule a video call and use Sections 2-6
3. **Reference Check:** Use Section 7 when contacting their references
4. **Decision:** Use their answers with the Contractor Evaluation Rubric

**Recommended Interview Length:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Section 1: Pre-Screen Questions (Send via Message)

Ask these questions before scheduling an interview to filter out unqualified candidates:

### Experience & Qualifications

**Q1:** How many platform profiles have you created or optimized in the last 12 months?
- *What you're looking for:* 20+ profiles shows consistent experience

**Q2:** Which of these 15 platforms have you worked with? (Provide your list)
- *What you're looking for:* Direct experience with at least 5-7 of your platforms

**Q3:** Can you share 2-3 examples of profiles you've created that resulted in measurable success? (Please include before/after or results data)
- *What you're looking for:* Specific, verifiable examples with outcomes

**Q4:** What is your typical turnaround time for a project of this scope (15 platforms)?
- *What you're looking for:* 3-6 weeks is realistic; under 2 weeks is a red flag

**Q5:** What is your pricing structure for this project? (Hourly vs. flat fee, payment schedule)
- *What you're looking for:* Clear, professional pricing in the $1,500-$2,500 range

## Section 2: Technical Expertise Questions

Ask these to assess their actual knowledge of platform optimization:

### Platform-Specific Knowledge

**Q6:** "Walk me through your process for optimizing an Upwork profile for maximum visibility."
- *What you're looking for:* Mention of keywords, title optimization, portfolio selection, client-centric language, success rate

**Q7:** "What are the top 3 mistakes you see people make on their LinkedIn profiles?"
- *What you're looking for:* Generic headlines, lack of keywords, no accomplishments, poor About section

**Q8:** "How does Braintrust differ from Upwork in terms of profile optimization strategy?"
- *What you're looking for:* Understanding that Braintrust is invite-only talent network vs open marketplace, different algorithms

**Q9:** "What's your strategy for passing Toptal's screening process through the application alone?"
- *What you're looking for:* Honesty that they can optimize the application but can't control the technical screening

**Q10:** "How do you optimize profiles for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) on job boards like Indeed and FlexJobs?"
- *What you're looking for:* Keywords, format, skills section, quantifiable achievements

### Writing & Content Strategy

**Q11:** "How do you adapt a client's professional summary for different platform audiences? Give me an example using a Business Systems Analyst."
- *What you're looking for:* Client-focused language for freelance platforms, accomplishment-focused for job boards, teaching-focused for education platforms

**Q12:** "Show me an example of a 'weak' project description and how you would strengthen it."
- *What you're looking for:* Before/after showing use of action verbs, quantifiable results, specific technologies

---

## Section 3: Process & Project Management Questions

Ask these to understand how they work:

### Workflow & Communication

**Q13:** "Walk me through your exact process from day 1 to project completion."
- *What you're looking for:* Structured process with clear milestones, check-ins, deliverables

**Q14:** "How often will you provide progress updates during the 4-week project?"
- *What you're looking for:* Weekly updates minimum, preferably twice per week

**Q15:** "What tools do you use to manage projects and communicate with clients?"
- *What you're looking for:* Professional tools (Trello, Asana, Slack, Google Workspace)

**Q16:** "How do you handle revisions? What's included in your base price?"
- *What you're looking for:* 2-3 rounds of revisions included, clear policy

**Q17:** "What happens if I'm not satisfied with a particular profile?"
- *What you're looking for:* Willingness to revise until satisfied within reason

### Security & Confidentiality

**Q18:** "How do you securely manage client login credentials and sensitive information?"
- *What you're looking for:* Password managers (1Password, LastPass), encrypted storage, NDA signing

**Q19:** "Are you willing to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and confidentiality clause?"
- *What you're looking for:* Immediate "yes" without hesitation

**Q20:** "What happens to my information after the project is complete?"
- *What you're looking for:* Secure deletion of credentials and personal data

---

## Section 4: Problem-Solving & Experience Questions

Ask these to assess critical thinking:

### Challenging Scenarios

**Q21:** "Tell me about a time when a platform rejected a client's application. How did you handle it?"
- *What you're looking for:* Problem-solving skills, client communication, learning from feedback

**Q22:** "What do you do when a client's work history or qualifications are weak for a particular platform?"
- *What you're looking for:* Honest assessment, strategic positioning, focus on strengths

**Q23:** "Have you ever had a project go over timeline? What happened and how did you resolve it?"
- *What you're looking for:* Accountability, problem-solving, client communication

**Q24:** "What's the most complex profile setup project you've completed?"
- *What you're looking for:* Similar scope or complexity to your project

### Results & Outcomes

**Q25:** "Can you share specific metrics or results from previous profile optimization projects?"
- *What you're looking for:* Profile views increased, job invitations, success rate improvements

**Q26:** "How do you measure the success of a profile you've created?"
- *What you're looking for:* Metrics like views, invitations, conversion rate, search ranking

---

## Section 5: Availability & Working Style Questions

Ask these to ensure compatibility:

### Logistics

**Q27:** "What is your current workload? How many clients are you working with right now?"
- *What you're looking for:* Not overbooked (under 5 active projects)

**Q28:** "What timezone are you in, and what are your typical working hours?"
- *What you're looking for:* Reasonable overlap for communication

**Q29:** "Do you work on weekends or have specific days off?"
- *What you're looking for:* Clear expectations about availability

**Q30:** "How quickly do you typically respond to client messages?"
- *What you're looking for:* Within 12-24 hours on business days

### Collaboration Style

**Q31:** "Do you prefer to work independently or do you like regular check-ins?"
- *What you're looking for:* Balance of autonomy and communication

**Q32:** "How do you handle feedback or requests for changes?"
- *What you're looking for:* Professional, non-defensive attitude

---

## Section 6: Business & Logistics Questions

Ask these to finalize terms:

### Contract & Payment

**Q33:** "What are your payment terms? (Upfront deposit, milestones, final payment)"
- *What you're looking for:* 50% upfront, 50% on completion is standard

**Q34:** "Do you provide a contract or Statement of Work before starting?"
- *What you're looking for:* Professional contractors should have a standard agreement

**Q35:** "What is included in your quoted price? What would be considered additional work?"
- *What you're looking for:* Clear scope, extras clearly defined

**Q36:** "Do you offer any guarantees or post-project support?"
- *What you're looking for:* 30-day support period, help with questions

### Expectations

**Q37:** "What do you need from me to get started, and when would you need it?"
- *What you're looking for:* Clear requirements list, reasonable timeline for you to provide materials

**Q38:** "Are there any platforms on my list that you haven't worked with before?"
- *What you're looking for:* Honesty about limitations, willingness to research

---

## Section 7: Reference Check Questions

Contact 2-3 of their previous clients and ask:

**Q39:** "How was [Contractor Name] to work with?"
- *What you're looking for:* Professional, communicative, reliable

**Q40:** "Did they complete the project on time and within budget?"
- *What you're looking for:* Yes, or reasonable explanation if no

**Q41:** "What was the quality of their work? Did you need many revisions?"
- *What you're looking for:* High quality, minimal revisions needed

**Q42:** "Did you see measurable results from the profiles they created?"
- *What you're looking for:* Increased visibility, job offers, invitations

**Q43:** "Would you hire them again?"
- *What you're looking for:* Enthusiastic yes

**Q44:** "Is there anything I should know before working with them?"
- *What you're looking for:* Honest feedback, any concerns

## Red Flag Answers - Walk Away If You Hear:

❌ "I can guarantee you'll get hired/accepted on all platforms"
❌ "I don't use contracts, we can just work on trust"
❌ "I need all the money upfront"
❌ "I can finish all 15 platforms in 3-5 days"
❌ "I don't provide revisions"
❌ "You don't need to give me your credentials, I'll create accounts with my own email"
❌ "I've never worked with most of these platforms, but I can figure it out"
❌ Vague, generic answers that don't demonstrate actual knowledge
❌ Defensive or aggressive when asked detailed questions
❌ Won't provide references or portfolio

---

## Green Flag Answers - Great Signs:

✅ Asks YOU detailed questions about your goals and background
✅ Provides specific examples and metrics from past work
✅ Acknowledges limitations honestly (e.g., "I can optimize your Toptal application, but their technical screening is up to you")
✅ Has a documented, structured process
✅ Offers to do a paid trial or sample (e.g., "Let me optimize just your LinkedIn first")
✅ Discusses strategy beyond just "filling out forms"
✅ Mentions platform-specific optimization tactics
✅ Professional in communication, responsive
✅ Willing to sign NDA and work with milestones
✅ Has an updated portfolio with before/after examples
