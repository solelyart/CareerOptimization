## Payment Terms & Conditions

### Client Responsibilities:
1. **Timely Review:** Review and approve/request revisions within 3 business days of milestone submission
2. **Clear Feedback:** Provide specific, actionable feedback if revisions are needed
3. **Prompt Payment:** Release payment within 24 hours of milestone approval
4. **Material Provision:** Provide all necessary materials (resume, photos, etc.) by project start date

### Contractor Responsibilities:
1. **Quality Work:** Deliver work that meets professional standards
2. **Timely Delivery:** Submit each milestone on or before the due date
3. **Responsive:** Respond to client questions within 12-24 hours
4. **Revisions:** Provide 2 rounds of revisions per milestone at no additional cost
5. **Confidentiality:** Keep all client information confidential and secure

### Revision Policy:
- **Included:** 2 rounds of revisions per milestone are included in the base price
- **Timeline:** Revisions will be completed within 2 business days
- **Scope:** Revisions must be within the original scope of work
- **Additional Revisions:** $50-$100 per hour for revisions beyond the 2 included rounds

### Late Delivery Policy:
- **Grace Period:** 1 business day grace period for minor delays
- **Extended Delay:** If contractor is 3+ days late without justification, client may:
  - Request a discount (10% of milestone payment)
  - Pause payment until work is complete
  - Cancel remaining milestones (with prorated refund if applicable)

### Dispute Resolution:
- **Communication First:** Both parties agree to communicate issues openly before escalating
- **Platform Mediation:** Use platform dispute resolution (Upwork, Fiverr) if needed
- **Partial Payment:** If project is incomplete, client will pay only for completed milestones
