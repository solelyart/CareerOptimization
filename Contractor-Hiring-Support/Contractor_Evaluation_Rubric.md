# Contractor Evaluation Rubric for Platform Setup Project

**Purpose:** Use this rubric to objectively evaluate and compare contractors/virtual assistants for your 15-platform profile setup project.

---

## How to Use This Rubric

1. **Score each contractor** on a scale of 1-5 for each criterion (1 = Poor, 5 = Excellent)
2. **Weight:** Some criteria are weighted more heavily (noted with multipliers)
3. **Total Score:** Calculate the weighted total out of 100
4. **Decision:** The contractor with the highest score is your best choice

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

**Immediate Disqualifiers:**
- ❌ Requests payment outside of platform (Upwork/Fiverr)
- ❌ Asks for login credentials before contract is signed
- ❌ Plagiarized proposal or portfolio
- ❌ Refuses to sign NDA or confidentiality agreement
- ❌ Cannot provide verifiable references
- ❌ Pressure tactics or guaranteed results that seem too good to be true
- ❌ Poor English in proposal if claiming native proficiency
- ❌ No response to direct questions about their process

## Interview Questions to Ask Top Candidates

Before making your final decision, ask these questions to your top 2-3 candidates:

1. "Can you walk me through your exact process for setting up a profile on [specific platform]?"
2. "What's the most challenging platform setup you've completed and why?"
3. "How do you stay current with algorithm changes on platforms like Upwork and LinkedIn?"
4. "What's your revision policy if I'm not satisfied with the content?"
5. "Can you provide a reference from a client with a similar project?"
6. "What tools do you use to manage passwords and credentials securely?"
7. "How will you ensure my profiles are optimized for search within each platform?"
8. "What happens if a platform rejects my application (e.g., Toptal screening)?"
