## Weekly Progress Meeting Agenda

Schedule a 15-30 minute video call every Friday to review progress.

### **Meeting Template:**

**Date:** [Every Friday]  
**Time:** [Your preferred time]  
**Platform:** Zoom / Google Meet / Phone Call

**Agenda:**

1. **Progress Update (5 min)**
   - What was completed this week?
   - Current status of each platform
   - Review Trello board together

2. **Quality Review (10 min)**
   - Quick review of 1-2 completed profiles
   - Discuss any issues or concerns
   - Provide immediate feedback

3. **Roadblocks & Questions (5 min)**
   - Any challenges or blockers?
   - Questions from contractor

   - What will be completed next week?
   - Any deadlines or priorities?
   - Client action items

   - Client to-dos

## Risk Management & Contingency Planning

### Common Risks & Mitigation:

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|------------|--------|---------------------|
| Contractor misses deadline | Medium | High | Use milestone payments; have backup contractor identified |
| Platform rejects application (Toptal, Chegg) | Medium | Medium | Understand this is outside contractor's control; no penalty |
| Client unavailable to review | Low | High | Set clear expectations; schedule reviews in advance |
| Quality below expectations | Low | High | Use detailed QA checklist; request revisions |
| Contractor disappears | Low | Very High | Use reputable platform (Upwork); milestone payments protect you |
| Budget overruns | Low | Medium | Fixed-price contract; clear scope in SOW |
