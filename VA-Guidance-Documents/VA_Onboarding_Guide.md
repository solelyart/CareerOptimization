# VA 7-Day Trial Onboarding Guide

This document outlines the day-by-day plan for the VA's first week (7-day trial).

## Day 1: Kickoff & Material Review

- **Morning:** 30-minute Kickoff Call (Introductions, Goal Review, Schedule Confirmation)
- **Afternoon:** VA to review all materials in the `CareerOptimization` repository, especially the `VA_Preparation_Guide_Optimized.md` and all strategy documents.

## Day 2: Foundational Profiles (Indeed & LinkedIn)

- **Task:** Set up profiles on Indeed and LinkedIn.
- **Goal:** Establish the foundational professional presence.

## Day 3: Tutoring Platforms (Wyzant & Chegg)

- **Task:** Set up profiles on Wyzant and Chegg.
- **Goal:** Complete applications for tutoring platforms.

## Day 4: Freelance Platforms (Upwork & Braintrust)

- **Task:** Set up profiles on Upwork and Braintrust.
- **Goal:** Showcase portfolio and freelance offerings.

## Day 5: Guided Practice Session & Course Platforms

- **Morning:** 60-minute Guided Practice Session (screen-share to review work and answer questions).
- **Afternoon:** Set up course shells on Teachable and Udemy.

## Day 6: Job Boards & Application Platforms

- **Task:** Set up profiles on FlexJobs and HigherEdJobs; begin applications for BELAY and Toptal.

## Day 7: Final Review & Wrap-Up

- **Morning:** VA to complete any remaining profile setups.
- **Afternoon:** 30-minute Final Review Call to discuss results, review all profiles, and determine next steps.
