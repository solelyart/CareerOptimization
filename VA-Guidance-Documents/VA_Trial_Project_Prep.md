# VA Trial Project Preparation

This document outlines the preparation needed for the 7-day trial project.

## Task 34: Block Time for Kickoff Call

- **Action:** Send a 30-minute calendar invite to the VA for the first day of the trial.
- **Agenda:**
  - Introductions
  - Review project goals and SOW
  - Confirm 7-day trial schedule
  - Answer initial questions

## Task 35: Block Time for Guided Practice Session

- **Action:** Send a 60-minute calendar invite for Day 5 of the trial.
- **Agenda:**
  - VA shares screen and walks through a completed profile.
  - You provide feedback and answer questions.
  - Jointly work on one profile setup to ensure alignment.

## Task 36: Block Time for Final Review Call

- **Action:** Send a 30-minute calendar invite for the last day of the trial.
- **Agenda:**
  - Review all 15 completed profiles.
  - Discuss what went well and any challenges.
  - Determine next steps for the ongoing engagement.

## Task 37: Prepare to Be Available for Questions

- **Action:** Decide on the best way for the VA to communicate with you.
- **Recommendation:** Use a dedicated Slack channel or the messaging system within your project tracking tool (Trello/Asana).
- **Set Expectations:** Let the VA know your expected response time (e.g., "I will respond to all questions within 4 business hours").
