## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the template** that matches your target role
2. **Customize** the bracketed [sections] with specific details
3. **Research** the company and add 1-2 specific references
4. **Proofread** carefully before submitting
5. **Save** each customized version for your records

**Pro Tips:**
- Always address by name if possible (check LinkedIn, company website)
- Keep it to 3-4 paragraphs maximum
- Lead with your strongest achievement
- End with a clear call to action
- Match keywords from the job description

## Customization Checklist

Before sending any cover letter, ensure you've:

- [ ] Addressed it to the right person (check LinkedIn, company website)
- [ ] Mentioned the company name at least 2-3 times
- [ ] Referenced a specific detail you researched about the company
- [ ] Matched 3-5 keywords from the job description
- [ ] Led with your strongest, most relevant achievement
- [ ] Quantified your results with metrics
- [ ] Kept it to 3-4 paragraphs (250-400 words max)
- [ ] Ended with a clear call to action
- [ ] Proofread for typos and grammar errors
- [ ] Used the same professional tone as their job posting
- [ ] Saved your customized version with naming convention: [Company]_[Position]_CoverLetter_[Date].pdf
