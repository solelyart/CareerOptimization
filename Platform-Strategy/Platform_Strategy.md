# Platform-Specific Strategy: Tutoring, Freelance, and Courses

This document outlines the strategy for platforms like Wyzant, MentorCruise, Upwork, Braintrust, Teachable, and Udemy.

## Tutoring Strategy (Wyzant, Chegg, MentorCruise)

**Subjects to Offer:**
- Business Analysis
- Project Management (Agile & Scrum)
- Salesforce CRM Basics
- SQL for Business
- Career Development for Tech

**Target Audience:**
- Early-career professionals (0-3 years experience)
- Career-changers looking to enter the tech industry
- College students in business or information systems programs

**Hourly Rate:**
- **Starting Rate:** $65/hour (to build initial reviews)
- **Standard Rate:** $85/hour (after 5-10 positive reviews)
- **Premium Rate:** $125/hour (for specialized topics like Salesforce career coaching)

## Freelance Services Strategy (Upwork, Braintrust, LinkedIn ProFinder)

**Core Service Offerings:**
1. **Salesforce CRM Health Check:** A one-time audit of a company's Salesforce instance with a report of findings and recommendations.
2. **Business Process Mapping:** A project to analyze and document a specific business process, delivering detailed workflow diagrams and an optimization plan.
3. **Agile Project Management Setup:** A package to help a small team set up their project management tools (Jira/Trello) and processes (Scrum/Kanban).

**Hourly Consulting Rate:**
- **Base Rate:** $90/hour (for general business analysis tasks)
- **Standard Rate:** $125/hour (for core service offerings)
- **Premium Rate:** $175/hour (for urgent projects or complex Salesforce strategy)

**Top Portfolio Pieces to Showcase:**
1. Revenue Operations Transformation
2. Contract Lifecycle Management Implementation
3. Financial Reporting Automation

## Course Creation Strategy (Udemy, Teachable)

**Primary Course Topic:**
- **Title:** "The Ultimate Guide to Becoming a Business Systems Analyst"
- **Subtitle:** "Land Your First Tech Job by Mastering Requirements, Processes, and Agile"

**Target Audience:**
- Aspiring Business Analysts with 0-2 years of experience
- Professionals in non-tech roles looking to transition into a tech career

**Course Outline (High-Level):**
- **Module 1:** What is a Business Systems Analyst?
- **Module 2:** Core Competencies: The BSA Toolkit
- **Module 3:** The Art of Requirements Gathering
- **Module 4:** Business Process Modeling 101
- **Module 5:** Agile, Scrum, and the BSA
- **Module 6:** From Analyst to Strategist: Career Growth
- **Module 7:** Landing Your First BSA Role

**Course Price Point:**
- **Launch Price:** $99 (with a 50% discount for the first month)
- **Standard Price:** $199
