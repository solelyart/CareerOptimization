## 1. Project Overview

This Statement of Work outlines the engagement between [Your Name/Company Name] (the "Client") and [Contractor/Virtual Assistant Name] (the "Contractor") for the comprehensive setup and optimization of professional profiles across 15 key online platforms. The goal of this project is to establish a consistent, professional, and highly visible online presence for the Client, tailored to the unique requirements and best practices of each platform.

This project will leverage the Client's existing professional materials (resume, CV, portfolio, etc.) to create compelling and fully-realized profiles on platforms spanning freelance marketplaces, job boards, educational sites, and corporate hiring portals.

---

## 2. Scope of Work

The scope of this project is divided into five categories, based on the type of platform. The Contractor will be responsible for all activities listed under each category for the specified platforms.

### 2.1 Self-Service Registration Platforms

**Platforms:** Wyzant, Braintrust, Udemy, Teachable, MentorCruise, Chegg

- **Account Creation:** Securely create a user account on each platform using the Client-provided email address.
- **Profile Completion:** Fill out all sections of the user profile, including but not limited to: personal information, professional summary/bio, work experience, education, and skills.
- **Content Optimization:** Adapt the Client's provided bio and resume to be keyword-rich and tailored to the platform's audience and search algorithms.
- **Media Uploads:** Upload a professional headshot, portfolio items, work samples, and any other required media.
- **Course/Service Shell Setup:** For platforms like Udemy and Teachable, create the initial shell for a course or service offering, including title, description, and pricing structure, based on Client input.

### 2.2 Job Board Platforms

**Platforms:** FlexJobs, HigherEdJobs, Indeed

- **Account & Profile Creation:** Create user accounts and complete all profile fields.
- **Resume/CV Upload & Parsing:** Upload the Client's resume/CV and ensure it is parsed correctly by the platform's system. Manually correct any parsing errors.
- **ATS Optimization:** Optimize the online profile and uploaded resume for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) by using relevant keywords and formatting.
- **Job Alert Configuration:** Set up customized job alerts based on the Client's specified criteria (e.g., job titles, industries, locations, salary ranges).

### 2.3 Company Hiring Process Platforms

**Platforms:** BELAY, University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC)

- **Application Form Completion:** Fill out all required fields in the online application forms with complete accuracy.
- **Document Upload:** Upload all necessary supporting documents, such as resume, CV, cover letter, transcripts, and certifications.
- **Thorough Review:** Double-check all submitted information for accuracy and completeness before final submission.

### 2.4 Rigorous Screening Platforms

**Platforms:** Toptal, Upwork

- **Application & Profile Creation:** Complete the initial application and create a compelling profile that highlights the Client's expertise.
- **Portfolio Curation:** Advise on the selection of the strongest portfolio pieces and work samples to showcase the Client's skills.
- **Skill Tagging & Description Writing:** Write detailed and persuasive descriptions of the Client's skills and experience, using platform-specific terminology.

> **Important Note:** The Contractor's role is to prepare and optimize the application materials to the highest possible standard. The Contractor is not responsible for the Client passing the platforms' internal screening tests, which are based on the platforms' own subjective evaluation criteria.

### 2.5 Course/Instructor & Service Platforms

**Platforms:** Coursera, LinkedIn Services Marketplace

- **Coursera:** Research and document the partnership process for becoming a Coursera content provider. The deliverable will be a summary of the requirements and steps involved, as direct individual instructor sign-up is not available.
- **LinkedIn Services Marketplace:** Create a compelling and detailed "Service Page" on the Client's LinkedIn profile, highlighting their freelance service offerings, expertise, and pricing.

## 3. Project Deliverables

Upon completion of the project, the Contractor will provide the following deliverables:

1.  **Master Credentials Spreadsheet:** A secure spreadsheet containing the login credentials (username, password) for all 15 platform accounts.
2.  **Profile Links Report:** A document with direct URLs to each of the 15 completed profiles for the Client's review.
3.  **Optimized Content Document:** A document containing the final, optimized text (e.g., bios, summaries, skill descriptions) used for each platform.
4.  **Final Project Report:** A summary of the work completed, including any platform-specific notes, observations, or recommendations for next steps (e.g., "Toptal requires a coding challenge to be completed within 48 hours of application approval").
