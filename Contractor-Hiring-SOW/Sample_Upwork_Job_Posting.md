## Expected Response

**Number of Proposals:** 15-30 within 48 hours (Upwork typically generates good response for profile optimization jobs)

**Quality Distribution:**
- 40% will be low-quality (generic, offshore, unqualified)
- 40% will be decent (some experience, reasonable proposals)
- 20% will be excellent (strong portfolio, detailed proposals, great fit)

**Your Goal:** Identify the top 3-5 from the excellent category and interview them.

**Timeline:**
- Day 1: Post job
- Day 2-3: Review proposals (expect 15-30)
- Day 4-5: Interview top 3-5 candidates
- Day 6: Select winner, send SOW
- Day 7: Signed SOW received, pay Milestone 1, project begins

## What Happens After Posting

**Expect:**
1. Proposals start coming in within 1-2 hours
2. You'll get notifications via email and Upwork app
3. Some proposals will be auto-generated (freelancers use AI) - skip these
4. You might get questions from candidates - answer promptly
5. Upwork will suggest freelancers - you can invite them to apply

**Your Actions:**
1. Check proposals daily (2x per day in first 3 days)
2. Mark best ones as "Maybe" or "Shortlist"
3. Decline obvious no-fits to keep your dashboard clean
4. Message top candidates to schedule interview
5. Use Contractor_Evaluation_Rubric.md to score each candidate
