## Quality Assurance Process

Every profile will go through my 3-stage QA process:

**Stage 1: Content Review**
- Grammar and spelling check (using Grammarly Premium)
- Fact consistency check (dates, titles, companies match across platforms)
- Keyword optimization verification
- Tone appropriateness for platform

**Stage 2: Technical Review**
- All fields 100% complete
- Links working correctly
- Images uploaded properly
- Account settings configured
- Test login successful

**Stage 3: Strategic Review**
- Is the content compelling?
- Would I hire/interview this person based on this profile?
- Does it differentiate the client from competitors?
- Are CTAs clear?
- Are results/achievements prominently featured?

Only after passing all 3 stages will I submit a platform for your review.

## Evaluation Guide for Kristen

When you receive proposals from contractors, look for:

**âœ… Strong Proposals Will Include:**
- Specific experience with multiple platforms on your list
- Concrete examples with results/metrics
- Clear timeline and process
- Transparent pricing
- References or testimonials
- Professional, error-free writing (this is crucial!)
- Proactive questions that show they've read your requirements carefully
- Understanding of your industry (Business Systems Analysis)

**ðŸš© Red Flags to Watch For:**
- Generic, copy-paste proposals ("I am very experienced professional...")
- No specific examples or portfolio
- Guaranteed results ("I guarantee you will get hired on Toptal")
- Suspiciously low pricing (under $1,000) from supposedly experienced contractors
- Poor grammar/spelling in the proposal itself
- Vague timelines or process
- Requests for payment outside of platform (if hiring through Upwork/Fiverr)
- Unwilling to provide references

**ðŸ’¡ Questions to Ask During Interview:**
- "Walk me through your exact process for setting up an Upwork profile."
- "What's your strategy for optimizing content for ATS systems on job boards?"
- "How do you ensure consistency across multiple platforms?"
- "Can you share a before/after example of a profile you optimized?"
- "What's your approach if a client's experience is strong but not differentiated?"
