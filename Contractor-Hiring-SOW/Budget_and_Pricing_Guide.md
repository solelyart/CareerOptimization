# Budget & Pricing Guide: Profile Setup Services

This guide provides a sample pricing structure that can be adapted by the Contractor. It includes options for hourly rates and a flat project fee, along with a breakdown of estimated hours.

---

## Option 1: Flat Project Fee

A flat fee provides cost certainty for the Client and rewards efficiency for the Contractor.

**Total Project Fee: $1,500 - $2,500**

*   **Lower End ($1,500):** Appropriate for a contractor who is highly experienced and has established templates and workflows for these platforms.
*   **Higher End ($2,500):** Appropriate for a contractor who will be developing materials from scratch and providing more in-depth strategic consultation for each platform.

**Payment Schedule:**
-   **50% Upfront Deposit:** $750 - $1,250
-   **50% Final Payment:** $750 - $1,250 (upon project completion)

---

## Option 2: Hourly Rate

An hourly rate is suitable if the scope is likely to change or if the Client wishes to have more flexibility in adding or removing tasks.

**Suggested Hourly Rate: $40 - $75/hour**

*   **$40/hour:** Entry-level to mid-level Virtual Assistant.
*   **$75/hour:** Experienced professional with specialized knowledge in profile optimization and career coaching.

### Estimated Time Breakdown

This table provides an estimate of the hours required for each platform category. Total estimated hours: **30-40 hours**.

| Platform Category | Platforms | Estimated Hours | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Self-Service Registration** | Wyzant, Braintrust, Udemy, Teachable, MentorCruise, Chegg | 10 - 14 hours | These require significant content adaptation and, in some cases, setting up initial course/service shells. Chegg's verification can also be time-consuming. |
| **Job Board Platforms** | FlexJobs, HigherEdJobs, Indeed | 6 - 8 hours | Focus is on resume parsing, ATS optimization, and configuring detailed job alerts. |
| **Company Hiring Processes** | BELAY, UMGC | 4 - 6 hours | These are formal applications that require meticulous data entry and document management. |
| **Rigorous Screening Platforms** | Toptal, Upwork | 6 - 8 hours | These platforms require the most strategic and persuasive writing to pass the initial screening. Portfolio curation is key. |
| **Course/Service Platforms** | Coursera, LinkedIn | 2 - 3 hours | Coursera is research-only. LinkedIn Service Page setup is relatively quick but requires strong copywriting. |
| **Project Management & Reporting** | N/A | 2 - 4 hours | Includes kick-off, weekly progress reports, and final deliverable preparation. |
| **Total Estimated Hours** | **15 Platforms** | **30 - 40 hours** | |

## Additional Costs (Client Responsibility)

Please note that the project fee does not include any third-party costs. These are to be covered directly by the Client.

| Platform | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **FlexJobs** | ~$25/month | Subscription required to create a full profile. |
| **Other Platforms** | Varies | Some platforms may have optional premium memberships that the Client may wish to purchase. |
